How to Use This System

Follow these steps to work with your counter-argument:

  1. Read the topic and select your stance.
  1. Write your counter-argument in your own words.
  1. Review the system's proposed structure for your counter-argument.
  2. If needed, browse other candidate structures.
  1. Start the feedback discussion to refine your reasoning.
  1. Revise your counter-argument based on the feedback.

Take your time and be as clear and specific as possible.
