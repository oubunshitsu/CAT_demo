How to Use This System

Follow these steps to work with your counter-argument:

  1. Read the topic and select your stance.
  2. Write your counter-argument in your own words.
  3. Discuss your counter-argument with the agent to refine it.
  4. Revise your counter-argument based on the feedback.

Take your time and be as clear and specific as possible.
